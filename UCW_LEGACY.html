Why Canada · Why Vancouver

A launchpad for ambition.

When your students ask "Why Canada?", UCW gives you a compelling answer. Studying in Vancouver places them inside one of North America's fastest-growing tech and startup hubs — with real pathways to work, build, and stay.

🏙️ A Top Startup Ecosystem

In 2025, Vancouver ranked among the top 15 startup ecosystems in North America and top 40 worldwide — home to Amazon, Salesforce, Samsung and a thriving founder community your students can plug into.

💼 Work While You Study

Eligible international students can work up to 24 hours a week, and UCW's practicum program connects MBA students with 200+ employers for real Canadian work experience before they graduate.

📈 Strong Job Market

British Columbia projects over 1,000,000 job openings through 2034. For students who qualify, post-study work permit pathways exist under IRCC criteria — a genuine route to launch a career.

🌎 Safe & Welcoming

Canada ranks among the world's safest and most welcoming countries, with Vancouver offering an unmatched quality of life — coastal beauty, diversity, and a truly global student community.
