Context · North India polymer opportunity (re: #14)

North India accounts for ~23% of national plastic & polymer consumption. India's plastics-processing market is ≈ US$26.6bn (₹3,938–4,100bn), implying a North India market of roughly US$6.1bn. V · New Business — GradAdvance

A separate company.

Low-residency onshore recruitment into DBA & PhD pathways — ring-fenced from InUni to protect the core proposition.

The Proposition

DBA & PhD pathway for F-1 students blocked from a 2nd master's.

Institution-to-institution UK mobility at low residency.

InUni legacy student database becomes a warm outreach pipeline.

Operationally independent from InUni, preserving brand integrity on both sides.

Market Opportunity
667K+Active US F-1 master's records (SEVIS 2024, +9.7% YoY)
41%US F-1 visa refusal rate (FY24, 10-yr high)

F-1 visas down 36% in 2025 (US Dept of State). Proposed DHS rule would ban 2nd master's for F-1 — forcing 667K+ toward DBA/PhD alternatives. Most agents hold no DBA/PhD contracts; GradAdvance fills this gap.

Canada → USA Pathway
1 · Canada
Non-PAL. Sem 1 full-time. Study permit (no F-1 required).
2 · US Enrol
SEVIS transfer. Day-1 CPT. Prior sems count toward 1-yr rule.
3 · FT CPT
From Day 1. 1 class/month. Up to 9 months.
4 · OPT/H-1B
<12 mo FT CPT preserves OPT. STEM OPT = 3 yrs.
Influencer & DSO

Joint IG with DSOs & immigration advisors; follower-growth referral.

Agent Network

Offshore agents (no direct uni contracts) become the GradAdvance channel.

GlobCred Finance

Tuition lending embedded in the enrolment journey.

InUni Legacy

Lead capture on partner sites + legacy database activation.
