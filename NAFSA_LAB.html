Our Method · How We Work

Find the friction. Build the system around it.

We work with operators — counsellors, admissions, compliance, partnerships — to find the friction first. Every component of InUni SMART started as a question on the operations floor.

01
Listen on the floor

We start with operators. Where does the work hurt? Where is judgment being spent on the wrong thing?

02
Frame the system problem

We separate the resourcing question from the structural one. More people don't fix inconsistency — they scale it.

03
Build inside the workflow

Tools are designed into the live operation, not bolted on. Every output is auditable, regenerable, and useful.

04
Measure, then iterate

We track hours returned, variation removed, time saved. If a tool isn't earning its place, we rebuild or retire it.

InUni / Suitability · Inside the workflow

7,592

hours saved · 2025
Scroll to explore
Step 01 · Capture

Applicant profile.

Applicants upload their CV and SOP alongside core details — one structured intake for every market. Every file lands in the same shape, ready for review.

Step 02 · Guide

Authentic essays.

Feedback highlights what each university is looking to see — applicants address it in their own voice, with AI-content detection on submission. Structural feedback only; the work belongs to the applicant.

Step 03 · Prepare

Interview readiness.

Interactive briefings on the city, programmes, and visa rules — applicants build a personal blueprint before the interview. By the time they sit down, they know what they're walking into.

Step 04 · Verify

Identity confirmed.

Live passport and face capture confirm the right person is sitting the interview — no proxies, no surprises at enrolment. The decision and the candidate are linked from the start.

Step 05 · Assess

Structured interviews.

An AI-led conversation runs the same structured assessment for every applicant — reviewers receive a consistent, pre-assessed outcome. Variation is removed before judgment is applied.

One workflow. Five components. Every applicant moves through the same standardised pipeline — across 21 countries and 11 time zones.

InUni / CreditMap · Inside the workflow

extract → output

Step 01 · Upload

Documents in.

Sending and receiving institutions named, syllabi, learning outcomes and study plans uploaded as PDFs. Custom extraction rules added where needed.

Step 02 · Extract

Courses parsed.

Course codes, credits, learning outcomes, and Bloom's levels pulled from each document automatically. The articulation evidence base is built before any mapping begins.

Step 03 · Review

Outcomes verified.

Every extracted course shown side-by-side — credits, Bloom's level, key outcomes editable before the engine runs. Human oversight on the inputs, not just the results.

Step 04 · Map

Equivalency resolved.

CLO-to-CLO overlap measured against set % thresholds with Bloom's and credit-volume checks. Each pairing returned as direct match, partial match, deficit, or surplus — with rationale and conditions attached.

Philosophy

The system handles scale. People handle judgment.

Three principles guide every component we build — and every one we retire.

P.01
Fairness by Standardisation

Same criteria. Every applicant. Every market. The structural answer to inconsistency.

P.02
Academic Integrity by Design

Structural feedback only. Student voice is never rewritten. The work belongs to the applicant.

Human Judgment at the Point of Decision

AI absorbs volume. Professionals decide. Expertise is preserved for the moments that need it.
