Universal exemptions (all markets)
Applicants who completed a bachelor's or master's degree in an English-speaking country are fully exempt from POE for PG applications.
Applicants whose bachelor's degree was taught entirely in English medium may submit a Medium of Instruction (MOI) letter in lieu of IELTS (PG only).
Applicants who completed their degree in an English-speaking country are also exempt, regardless of where the institution is based.
Agent reminders
Always verify the exemption evidence is present in the file before submission — missing mark sheet percentage, unsigned MOI letter, or incorrect WAEC grade will delay the application. Do not assume; always check.
