Convocation Overview
About This Convening

The INUNI Grand Convocation on Global Higher Education brings together the most distinguished minds in international higher education for two days of rigorous intellectual exchange, strategic dialogue, and relationship formation. Hosted by INUNI at GUS Group institutions in London, the Convocation is modelled on the great academic gatherings of the world's leading research universities — intimate in scale, exceptional in calibre, consequential in outcome.

The programme encompasses in-depth case studies of exemplary TNE partnerships, thought leadership panels with global scholars, peer roundtable discussions, institutional showcases, and focused academic workshops — culminating in an invitation to join INUNI and its partners at the prestigious PIE (Professionals in International Education) Awards Gala on 4th September, where the sector's most celebrated achievements are honoured in the tradition of the great academic prize-giving ceremonies.

The Grand Gala
Dinner & Awards Night
In Proud Association with
The PIE Awards 2026
Professionals in International Education

As the curtain falls on two days of extraordinary intellectual discourse, INUNI invites every Convocation delegate to be present for the crowning ceremony of the international education calendar: a formal Gala Dinner and Awards Night of the highest distinction, hosted in one of London's most magnificent venues.

The evening opens with a champagne reception in gilded surroundings, where the world's leading education figures assemble in the spirit of shared endeavour. A seated five-course dinner of the finest culinary craft follows — before the grand hall is hushed for the presentation of the PIE Awards, the most coveted honours in international and transnational education.

To be present in that room is to stand among the architects of the future of higher education — laureates, leaders, and luminaries from every continent — united in the conviction that education, pursued across borders and across cultures, remains among the noblest of human endeavours.

The Honours of the Evening

The Highest Honour in the Sector

The PIE Awards are to international education what the Nobel Prize is to science — the singular, peer-validated affirmation that one has not merely participated in the advancement of global learning, but has irrevocably shaped it.

A Night of Historic Distinction

Established in the tradition of the great academic prize-giving ceremonies, the Gala Dinner is an occasion that laureates recall throughout their careers — a moment of formal recognition before the peers whose respect they have earned.

Judged by the World's Foremost Authorities

Every nomination is assessed by an independent panel of internationally recognised leaders in higher education, ensuring that each award carries the full weight of sector-wide credibility, scholarly rigour, and unimpeachable integrity.

An Unforgettable Evening

From the first clink of champagne to the final ovation, the Gala is an evening of theatre, beauty, and genuine emotion — where the achievements of a lifetime are honoured before the audience that matters most.
